    ingredients

  • 3 cups of Hibiscus flowers
  • 1.5 cup of pineaple juice
  • 5 onces of grind ginger
  • 1 cup of mint leaves
  • 1 Tbs of vanilla
  • 18 cups of water

To make the Hibiscus juice

  1. Clean the Hibiscus flowers and the mint leaves
  2. Put the flower and the mint leaves in a pot and boil it for 20 minutes
  3. After it finish boiling, let it cool down first before moving to the next step
  4. Pour it into a sieve on a bowl and throw the Hibiscus flowers and mint leaves into the trash
  5. Pour a 1 liter of water and the liquid pineaple and the vinalla into the pot
  6. put the ginger into the pot and stir it for 2 minutes then put it into the refrigerator
