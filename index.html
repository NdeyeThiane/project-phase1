Hibiscus juice (Bissap)

Hibiscus juice is a really healthy drink. It's high in vitamin C so it's good for your immune system and gut health.

  • 3 cups of Hibiscus flowers
  • 1.5 cup of pineaple juice
  • 5 onces of ginger
  • 1 cup of mint leaves
  • 18 cups of water

To make the Hibiscus juice

  1. Clean the Hibiscus flowers and the mint leaves
  2. Put the flower and the mint leaves in a pot and boil it for 20 minutes
  3. After it finish boiling, let it cool down first beforre moving to the next step
